About Horizon Aid

Built with communities, not just for them

Horizon Aid International partners directly with local leaders to design programs that outlast us. Every initiative is co-designed with the communities it serves, measured openly, and built to create lasting impact long after we've moved on.

  • Locally-led program design in every region we serve
  • Independent annual audits and public impact reports
  • Aligned to the UN Sustainable Development Goals
